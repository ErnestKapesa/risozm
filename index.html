About RISO

RISO means "ideal" in Japanese, chosen by our founder Noboru Hayama to reflect our commitment to excellence. Since 1954, RISO has been at the forefront of innovation in paper-based communication technology.

We strive to create fundamentally unique technologies that emphasize:

  • Productivity
  • Cost containment
  • Versatility

Present in over 150 countries, RISO continues to lead the world in digital duplicating technology while expanding our digital laser and inkjet printing solutions.
